# Introduction

## NUS Formula SAE Team
<p style='text-align: justify'>
The NUS Formula Society of Automotive Engineers (FSAE) Team is an engineering project under the Engineering Design and Innovation Centre (EDIC) comprising Electrical and Mechanical Engineering students. A total of 27 team members make up the R26E team, consisting of 13 fourth year students and 14 third year students, who will be building the R26E race car.
